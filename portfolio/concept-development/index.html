Concept Development

All products start with an idea. If that idea can be captured in a way that’s tangible and clear, the concept can be improved, collaborated on, and momentum can be built.

An important part of crystalising concepts, is to begin to logically group ideas and place them inside a broader context. This helps generate further ideas, makes the concept easier to grasp and sell, and identifies scope leading to improved planning outcomes.
