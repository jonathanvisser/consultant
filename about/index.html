About me

I am a consultant with a broad range of experience from low level technical work, business analysis, and data modelling – specialising in technical BA and product management.

I’m interested in working with ambitious, cutting-edge, innovative teams who are dedicated to delivering the best possible outcomes to their customers - and who need help crystallising concepts into clear, actionable plans, and high-quality solutions.

I bring a broad range of experience covering technical, creative, business, and customer areas. I enjoy dynamic environments, where change and evolution are business-as-usual.

I’ve worked on a range of innovative products - taking ideas from conception to delivery - working closely with customers at the same time as putting in place formal processes to support high-quality results.

My experience makes me good at picking the right approach to each challenge - and at identifying business opportunities whilst optimising value in the current project.
